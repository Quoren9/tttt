About TWTL

TWTL is a non-governmental philanthropic organization with the goal of ensuring consistency in sharing positive energy through humanitarian service, entrepreneurship training, and actualization of dreams.

TWTL Scope

Humanitarian services: One of the prominent goals of TWTL is to harness the trends in this dispensation by playing a key role in the work against social issues like depression, racism, tribalism, and gender with public and private organizations.

Entrepreneurship: Another major role of TWTL is harnessing talents through entrepreneurship programs. This is carried out through innovative ideas, problem-solving skills, and collaboration with public and private organizations.

Education: TWTL values education. It helps students by contributing to their school needs and providing for the success of their academic pursuits. Some of these include school items like bags, books, uniforms, and sanitary pads for female students.

Dream actualization: TWTL serves the purpose of bringing the impossible into possibilities. To enlighten young, youth, and aged on the imperative nature of having a dream, and developing dreams into reality.
